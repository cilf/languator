Languator

Aims to help with English synonyms, derived words, autosuggestion and spelling easily and efficiently.

Textarea

  • Suggests words as you type. Enter or Tab inserts the word.
  • CtrlKey + . (dot key) opens up additional menu.
  • You can pop up suggestions with CtrlKey + Space.

Infobox

  • You can move around holding CtrlKey (Up, Down, Left, Right).
  • You can insert the word with Enter, Tab or LeftClick.
  • You can show synonyms for the word with CtrlKey + LeftClick.
  • Is movable and resizable.
  • Remembers it's size and position.

There's also demonstration using WYMeditor.
